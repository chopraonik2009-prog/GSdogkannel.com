Our History

Over Two Decades of Uncompromising Standards

At GS Dog Kannel, we began with a simple but profound mission: to elevate the standards of dog breeding in India. Guided by Kannel Club of India (KCI) certification rules, we choose only the most outstanding bloodlines to ensure genetic integrity, structural soundness, and stable temperaments.

Our modern breeding facility is designed as a sanctuary. Unlike conventional kannels, our dogs enjoy open exercising yards, temperature-controlled nursery suites, and custom-formulated diets created by leading veterinary nutritionists.
